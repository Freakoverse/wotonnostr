Web of Trust on Nostr visualized

A visual demonstration of a Nostr Web of Trust (WoT) system.
Assume you're already logged in on a nostr client, and you're setting up your WoT filter. 
All of these posts are published notes from people you don't follow, but a number of who you follow have either followed them, not followed them, or muted/blocked them.
Based on how you've set up your WoT filter, it would or wouldn't show posts. Hopefully this helps explain WoT on Nostr.

What's highlighted is what you'll see in your feed. What's not is what you won't see.
